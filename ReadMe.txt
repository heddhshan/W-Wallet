W-Wallet 第一个版本（20220903） 发布了!  (W-Wallet's First Version (20220903) is Released!)


W-Wallet 是一个专业的以太坊桌面钱包，功能强大，安全性高，适用于持币大户、交易所财务人员、炒币人员、发币团队和推广团队、区块链技术人员，等对区块链基础知识有一定了解的用户。
W-Wallet 是一个在线钱包，对应的还有一个离线钱包 W-Offline Wallet ，这两款钱包可以彼此配合，做到私钥和助记词离线保存，签名后的交易在线执行。

W-Wallet is a professional Ethereum desktop wallet with powerful functions and high security. It is suitable for large currency holders, exchange financial personnel, currency speculators, currency issuance and promotion teams, blockchain technicians, and peer-to-peer areas Users who have a certain understanding of blockchain basics.
W-Wallet is an online wallet, and there is a corresponding offline wallet W-Offline Wallet. These two wallets can cooperate with each other to save the private key and mnemonic offline, and execute the signed transaction online.


这两个钱包DApp是系列的一部分，其下载支持去中心化，打开菜单[Dapp Client List]即可看见所有的Dapp下载链接。
整个DApp系列，都会支持多语言，一般原始语言是中文或英文，译文语言支持全球几百种语言，但是界面的排版只支持从左到右，从上到下。一般会提供中英文两种语言；各种译文可以实时增加和更新。

These two wallet DApps are part of the series, and their downloads support decentralization. Open the menu [Dapp Client List] to see all the Dapp download links.
The entire DApp series will support multiple languages. Generally, the original language is Chinese or English, and the translation language supports hundreds of languages around the world, but the layout of the interface only supports left-to-right and top-to-bottom. Generally, two languages, Chinese and English, are provided; various translations can be added and updated in real time.
